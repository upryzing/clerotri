# On iOS support

For a few reasons, I (Rexogamer) am unable to work on iOS support:

- I don't have a Mac, and for one specific purpose spending several hundred pounds on a MacBook/_maybe_ a Mac mini is very hard to justify,
- especially in comparison to Android, iOS seems to require a lot more finicky, manual maintenance (e.g. `.pbxproj` files), and,
- even if I _were_ to get my hands on a Mac and the maintenance burden was reduced, publishing iOS apps to the App Store (which, outside specific regions, is mandatory for anyone to be able to use the app) would require me to spend $99/year on a developer license.

For comparison, even with the awful developer verification program, putting out an APK that is at least somewhat usable globally is free, and - while I haven't done so for other reasons - publishing apps to the Play Store only requires a one-time fee of about £25, which is much more affordable.

I've left the folder and the (almost certainly broken) here to (hopefully) help anyone who wants to try and get it working again. I welcome you to give it a shot, but I can't guarantee that I'll be able to maintain or merge the changes without the ability to test and review the app myself. If a co-maintainer with the ability to do so emerges in the future, this may be re-assessed.

I also ask that, if you _do_ manage to get it working, you refrain from publishing it to the App Store without my consent. I'd be happy to hear of any progress or suggestions in [the Clerotri server](https://stt.gg/clerotri), though! I'd also be happy to accept and merge any changes for iOS support that either improve things on other platforms or are JS-side and easy to maintain :3
